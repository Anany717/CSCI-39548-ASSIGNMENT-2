I have developed and honed skills such as Python programming, assistant teaching, logistics management, business development, communication, and teamwork through my education and work experience. I am always eager to learn new things, challenge myself, and collaborate with others. I am passionate about computer science, education, and social good, and I aspire to use my skills and knowledge to make a positive difference in the world.
